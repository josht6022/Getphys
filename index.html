This is the Physics Refuge

Sometimes learning physics for the first time can leave you in the stars...literally. There's a necessity to redefine terms you've heard all you life, like momentum or power. These words mean something very different to physicist. However, with the right tools, physics is within anyone's reach. To be clear, this website is showing you only a small fraction of those tools. The other tools come with a great amount of energy devoted to understanding the material.
